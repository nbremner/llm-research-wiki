# AI-enabled job crafting topic-assessment note

Use this pattern when ingesting GenAI/AI-at-work sources that model AI usage as a worker-level job resource, especially with JD-R, job crafting, work engagement, autonomy, competence, or motivational mediators.

## Topic-map lesson

Do not flatten these sources into only `ai-mediated-work-experience`, `work-redesign`, or `employee-engagement` when the central mechanism is **bottom-up worker-led reshaping of work**. That construct deserves the more precise topic `ai-enabled-job-crafting` when the source claims that workers use AI to change tasks, workflows, effort allocation, work meaning, or resource/demand fit.

## Create/update/defer pattern

- **Create or update `ai-enabled-job-crafting`** when the source treats AI as a job resource that enables proactive task/work redesign by the worker, not merely adoption, attitude, or productivity.
- **Also update `ai-mediated-work-experience`** when the source links AI use to autonomy, competence, workload, engagement, motivation, identity, or felt work quality.
- **Also update `work-redesign`** when the implication is that informal worker-led redesign may precede formal role/workflow architecture and should be noticed by governance, measurement, or management systems.
- **Also update `employee-engagement`** only when the source uses academic work engagement or explicitly helps separate engagement from job crafting, AI usage, or performance. Avoid treating engagement as a generic positive outcome bucket.
- **Defer `digital-competence-as-ai-resource`** unless there are multiple sources distinguishing digital competence from AI literacy/readiness/self-efficacy as a moderator of worker-level AI value creation.

## Evidence cautions to preserve

For cross-sectional self-report PLS-SEM papers, state the nomological-network contribution without causal hardening. Treat strong R² values cautiously when all variables are survey self-reports. Note coefficient/table inconsistencies directly in the source record when present; they are evidence-quality limitations, not synthesis trivia.

## Source-feed governance reminder

If the source record was already auto-committed without a new-topic feed, add the new feed link only in the uncommitted topic-synthesis proposal and include that source-file patch in the approval package.
